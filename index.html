About Us

The Agriculture Department of India serves as the cornerstone of the nation’s agrarian economy, entrusted with the vital responsibility of ensuring food security, promoting rural development, and driving agricultural growth. As the principal agency shaping agricultural policies, the department works to create a robust and sustainable framework that empowers farmers and uplifts rural communities. With over half of the Indian population relying on agriculture for their livelihood, our mission is to enhance productivity, promote equitable resource distribution, and mitigate challenges such as climate change and market fluctuations. Through policy interventions, we aim to transform agriculture into a modern, technology-driven, and globally competitive sector, while preserving the heritage and diversity of India’s farming traditions. The department also acts as a bridge, linking farmers to national and international markets, research bodies, and technological advancements, fostering a dynamic ecosystem that propels rural prosperity.

Innovation and sustainability lie at the heart of our initiatives. The department is committed to harnessing cutting-edge technologies and scientific advancements to revolutionize Indian agriculture. From the promotion of precision farming, drip irrigation, and climate-resilient crop varieties to the implementation of sustainable practices like organic farming, our efforts are geared towards creating a balance between productivity and environmental stewardship. Research and development play a pivotal role in our strategy, with collaborations with leading agricultural universities and research institutions ensuring that farmers benefit from the latest breakthroughs. Our digital transformation efforts, including platforms like e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) and advanced mobile applications for weather forecasting and pest management, provide farmers with real-time solutions and market connectivity, reducing inefficiencies and enhancing their decision-making capabilities.

The Agriculture Department of India also places a strong emphasis on the socio-economic empowerment of farmers, recognizing their invaluable contribution to nation-building. Through flagship programs like PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi, soil health cards, and crop insurance schemes, we aim to alleviate financial burdens, improve agricultural practices, and secure farmers’ livelihoods against uncertainties. Special focus is given to marginalized communities and smallholder farmers, ensuring inclusivity and equitable access to resources. By driving sustainable rural development and aiming for the ambitious goal of doubling farmers’ income, the department contributes significantly to the vision of Atmanirbhar Bharat (self-reliant India). With an unwavering commitment to innovation, inclusivity, and sustainability, the Agriculture Department of India continues to be a transformative force, fostering a future where agriculture thrives as a pillar of national growth and global food security.
