1. Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering

Focusing on developing genetically modified crops to improve yield, pest resistance, and adaptability to diverse climatic conditions.

  • Development of high-yielding crop varieties.
  • Research on drought and pest-resistant crops.
  • Biotechnological advancements for sustainable farming.

2. Soil and Water Management

Enhancing soil fertility and optimizing water use to improve crop productivity sustainably.

  • Soil testing and nutrient management techniques.
  • Efficient water harvesting and irrigation systems.
  • Research on soil microbiology for better crop health.

3. Climate-Resilient Farming

Adapting farming practices to mitigate the impacts of climate change on agriculture.

  • Development of heat and drought-tolerant crop varieties.
  • Integrated pest and disease management solutions.
  • Research on carbon sequestration in farming.

4. Precision Agriculture

Leveraging advanced technologies for data-driven decision-making in agriculture.

  • Use of drones and sensors for crop monitoring.
  • GPS-enabled farming equipment for precision sowing.
  • Big data analytics for agricultural insights.

5. Post-Harvest Technology

Innovations to reduce post-harvest losses and improve the value chain.

  • Research on advanced storage and preservation techniques.
  • Development of efficient cold chain logistics.
  • Value addition through agro-processing technologies.

6. Sustainable Farming Practices

Promoting eco-friendly and sustainable methods of agriculture to conserve natural resources.

  • Organic farming research and promotion.
  • Agroforestry systems for biodiversity conservation.
  • Integrated farming systems for resource optimization.

7. Digital Agriculture

Incorporating digital tools to transform agricultural practices and farmer connectivity.

  • Mobile apps for farmer advisory services.
  • AI-powered platforms for predictive analysis.
  • Blockchain for transparent supply chain management.
